Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration and later the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, later became the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned inventor, diplomat, and statesman; key figure in securing French support during the Revolutionary War.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress; known for his large and bold signature.
Samuel Adams
A leader of the Sons of Liberty and influential in organizing resistance against British policies.
Roger Sherman
The only person to sign all four of the great state papers of the U.S.: the Continental Association, the Declaration of Independence, the Articles of Confederation, and the Constitution.

These individuals played major roles in shaping the early United States, both politically and symbolically.
